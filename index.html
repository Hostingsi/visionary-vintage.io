About us

History


The HR business located in Maseru, Lesotho, was established in 2019 with the aim of providing comprehensive human resource solutions to organizations in the region. The company has a strong focus on talent acquisition, training and development, performance management, and employee relations.

Objectives


The primary objective of the HR business is to assist organizations in optimizing their human capital to achieve strategic goals and objectives. This includes ensuring that companies have the right talent in place, developing employees to reach their full potential, and fostering a positive work environment conducive to productivity and growth.

Aims


The HR business aims to become a trusted partner for businesses in Lesotho by offering innovative HR solutions tailored to each client’s unique needs. By staying abreast of industry trends and best practices, the company strives to deliver value-added services that drive organizational success.

Organisational structure



The HR business in Maseru likely follows a traditional organizational structure common in HR consulting firms. This structure typically includes departments such as recruitment, training and development, compensation and benefits, employee relations, and HR administration. Each department is headed by a manager or director who reports to the CEO or managing director.

Team

Thato Kobisi

Recruiter: responsible for managing the hiring process from start to finish. Work closely with internal hiring managers to identify job requirements, create job descriptions, post openings on various platforms, source candidates through different channels like job fairs and social media, conduct interviews, collect feedback, negotiate offers, and communicate with new hires during onboarding.

Mpitsane Hlapisi

Employee relations specialist: focus on maintaining positive relationships between employees and the employer. They handle aspects related to employee satisfaction, corporate culture, conflict resolution, and overall employee engagement.

Ponts'o Thabana

Compensation and Benefits Specialist: Professionals in this role are responsible for evaluating pay practices of competitors, establishing compensation structures, creating job descriptions in collaboration with hiring managers, and working on succession planning with talent management teams.

Lineo Letsoela

Training and development coordinators focus on providing employees with the necessary tools and resources to enhance their skills and knowledge.

Nteboheleng Makharilele

HR generalist: is a versatile professional who handles a wide range of HR functions across different areas such as recruitment, employee relations, training, benefits administration, compliance with labor laws, policy implementation, performance management support, and more.
